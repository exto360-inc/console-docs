The Exto suite, one console

Operate Exto and every product in the suite — Bolo, Canvas, and Faria — from a single platform. No tab-switching between tools, no stitched-together views.

Complete customer view for account managers

Every tenant, instance, user, plan, meter, and invoice for a customer lives in one record. Account managers walk into reviews with full context.

Usage tracking across every product

Watch customer consumption in real time across meters, tenants, and instances. Spot growth, churn risk, and overage before they show up on an invoice.

Identity that spans every server

Add, suspend, or remove users across dev, QA, and production in a single action. Push security policies and user attributes to every environment from one screen.

Billing that scales with the business

Plans, meters, invoices, and payments run on autopilot. Revenue grows without growing the back office.

End-to-end release management

Cut major, minor, and hotfix releases from the console. Account managers and server admins approve promotions from dev → QA → prod, and customers see one consolidated changelog across every product they use.
