Exto Console: The Business Operating System ​

Executive Summary ​

As our business scales, our infrastructure and operational overhead must not scale at the same linear rate. Currently, critical stages of our customer lifecycle—provisioning instances, tracking API and storage usage, billing accurately, and managing identity—are disjointed.

Exto Console (formerly referred to as Exto Hub) is our proprietary, centralized SaaS Control Plane. It unifies billing, identity, infrastructure health, and release management into a single automated engine. It is the machine that runs the business, transforming manual DevOps and finance tasks into an automated, highly scalable workflow.

The Problem: The Invisible Ceiling ​

Scaling a B2B SaaS platform introduces compounding complexity. Without a centralized orchestration engine, we face an "invisible ceiling" capping our growth:

  1. Revenue Leakage: When usage tracking (API calls, storage, active users) lives in a different system than invoicing, we miss billing opportunities and leave money on the table.
  2. Operational Bottlenecks: Manually provisioning tenant databases, assigning licenses, and deploying software updates requires expensive engineering time.
  3. Disjointed Security & Identity: Every new application we launch requires us to reinvent the authentication wheel, increasing the surface area for security vulnerabilities and creating a disjointed user experience.
  4. Margin Compression: If we acquire 100 new enterprise customers, under an unautomated model, we must hire a proportional number of support and DevOps staff to manage them.

The Solution: Exto Console ​

Exto Console is the unified "nervous system" for our platform. It abstracts away operational complexity, providing our teams with a powerful console to manage the entire B2B lifecycle.

Key Pillars of Exto Console ​

  1. Universal Identity & Access (Driven by Zitadel)

    • Exto Console serves as the unified "Passport Control" for our entire ecosystem.
    • It provides a single, beautiful login and SSO experience for all our applications.
    • It binds physical environments to logical user permissions, ensuring rock-solid, multi-tenant security architecture.

  2. Automated Metering & Billing Engine

    • Automatically calculates usage based on custom meters (active_projects, api_calls, storage_gb).
    • Ties directly to customer plans and auto-generated invoices.
    • Prevents revenue leakage by eliminating manual billing computations.

  3. Infrastructure & Release Orchestration

    • Acts as the command center for our Kubernetes pods and database instances.
    • Tracks CPU, memory, and latency health dynamically.
    • Manages software versioning and pushes updates to fleets of instances systematically, eliminating "configuration drift" between clients.

  4. Tenant License Enforcement

    • Centralizes what level of software a tenant has paid for.
    • Automatically restricts user licensing counts and feature toggles (e.g., PowerBI dashboard access) without engineer intervention.

ROI: Why This Matters to the Board ​

We did not just build an administrative tool; we built a lever for business efficiency.

  • Capture Lost Revenue: By deeply integrating a metering system directly into our infrastructure, we guarantee that every byte of storage and every API call consumed by our customers is accurately billed according to their contract.
  • Reduce Operating Expenses (OPEX): We can now decouple our customer growth from our headcount growth. By automating the deployment and management of tenant environments, the same DevOps and Finance team can manage 10x the client load.
  • Accelerate Product Velocity: Because Exto Console handles SSO, user profiles, and billing globally, our product engineers never have to build these from scratch again. They can focus 100% of their time on building revenue-generating product features.
  • Enterprise-Grade Compliance: Exto Console’s strict identity mapping and centralized audit events (audit_events table) gives us the security posture required to close large enterprise contracts and pass SOC2 audits with ease.

Strategic Conclusion ​

Exto Console is our proprietary competitive advantage. It ensures that our infrastructure will never become the bottleneck for our sales and growth team. By adopting Exto Console as our absolute internal standard, we lay a foundation capable of scaling infinitely, securely, and profitably.
